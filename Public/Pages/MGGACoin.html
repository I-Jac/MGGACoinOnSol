How to Buy MGGA Coin

Step 1: Create a Wallet

To start, you'll need a Crypto wallet that supports Solana. Here are a few options:

  • Jupiter Wallet (Phone only)
  • Phantom Wallet
  • Solflare Wallet
  • Coinbase Wallet

Follow the instructions on the wallet's website to create and secure your wallet.

Step 2: Buy Solana

Purchase Solana (SOL) from an exchange or swapped. Some popular exchanges include:

  • Binance
  • Coinbase
  • Swapped

Transfer your SOL to your Crypto wallet once purchased.

Or buy SOL directly in your wallet - Find Web3 within your wallet and go to Jupiter Onramp

  • Jupiter Onramp

Step 3: Swap Solana for MGGA

Use a decentralized exchange (DEX) to swap your SOL for MGGA.

  • Jupiter Swap

Connect your wallet, select SOL and MGGA, and execute the swap.
